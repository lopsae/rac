    (static) horizontalAlign :object

    # | source: drawable/Text.Format.js, line 30

    Supported values for hAlign which dermines the left-to-right alignment of the drawn text in relation to its text.point.

    Members are:

    • left - aligns text.point to the left edge of the drawn text
    • center - aligns text.point to the center, from side to side, of the drawn text
    • right - aligns text.point to the right edge of the drawn text

    (static) verticalAlign :object

    # | source: drawable/Text.Format.js, line 51

    Supported values for vAlign which dermines the top-to-bottom alignment of the drawn text in relation to its text.point.

    Members are:

    • top - aligns text.point to the top edge of the drawn text
    • center - aligns text.point to the center, from top to bottom, of the drawn text
    • baseline - aligns text.point to the baseline of the drawn text
    • bottom - aligns text.point to the bottom edge of the drawn text
