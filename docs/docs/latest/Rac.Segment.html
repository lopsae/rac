    nextSegmentToAngleDistance(angleDistance, clockwiseopt, lengthopt, nullable) → {Rac.Segment}

    Returns a new Segment starting from endPoint() towards the given angleDistance from this.angle().inverse() in the clockwise orientation.

    The new Segment will have the given length, or when ommited or null will use this.length instead.

    Notice that the angleDistance is applied to the inverse of the segment's angle. E.g. with an angleDistance of 0 the resulting Segment will be directly over and pointing in the inverse angle of this. As the angleDistance increases the two segments separate with the pivot at endPoint().
